Slide Into Your Comfort Zone

HushMap helps you discover places with comfortable noise levels, lighting, and crowd sizes. Our app uses smart technology to predict how comfortable a place will be before you visit.

Built for Sensory Comfort

We understand that different environments affect people differently. HushMap is designed specifically for people who need predictable, comfortable spaces.

Main Features

AI Predictions

Smart predictions for noise levels, crowds, and lighting at any location using advanced AI and weather data.

Sound Meter

Built-in sound measurement to help you track and report noise levels in real-time with accurate decibel readings.

Interactive Maps

Color-coded maps showing comfortable places near you with Google Maps integration and easy navigation.

Personalization

The app learns your sensory preferences over time, making increasingly accurate recommendations tailored to you.

How It Helps You

HushMap reduces uncertainty about new places. Instead of wondering if a restaurant will be too loud or a store too crowded, you can check HushMap first.

The app learns from your feedback. When you tell us a place was comfortable or uncomfortable, our predictions become more accurate for you personally.

You can use HushMap without creating an account. Your privacy is protected, and you control what information you share.

Designed for Accessibility

HushMap works with screen readers and voice controls. Text size can be adjusted. Colors and animations are designed to be comfortable for people with sensory sensitivities.

The app uses clear, simple language and predictable navigation. Every feature is tested to work well for people with autism, ADHD, and other neurodivergent experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

HushMap is an AI-powered iOS app that helps you find quiet, comfortable places with ideal sensory conditions. It predicts noise levels, crowd density, and lighting at restaurants, stores, and other venues before you visit. The app is specifically designed for people with autism, ADHD, sensory processing differences, and anyone who prefers calmer environments.

Our AI predictions combine multiple data sources including real user reports, time patterns, weather conditions, and venue characteristics. Accuracy improves as more people use the app and share their experiences. Most predictions are 75-85% accurate, with confidence scores shown for each prediction.
